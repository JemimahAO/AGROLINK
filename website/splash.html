About Us

The Agrolink is a project to help farmers ensure greater profitability through direct farmer to supplier and farmer to farmer communication. This service boosts business communication and brings transparency in the system.

  • a community section in which farmer brothers from different states can interact with each other.
  • Minimal as well as sufficient so that anyone can easily use the site.
  • Contains the latest news regarding agriculture.

This farming assistance web service is designed and developed to overcome the drawbacks of the old manual system and meet the requirements of the modern age. this system will digitalize the agricultural industry and give more profit to farmers . It will bring transparency between farmer and supplier by removing intermediates. Will save energy and time of farmer and customer too. And this web service will be the link in agricultural industry.

The Agrolink Farmers Assistance Web service

Our project presents a new approach which will be appreciated by the end user. This is user friendliness as well as new features that were never seen in this field before such as a community blog. This is done in the interest of bringing people closer as well as giving them an environment in which the middle man is eliminated.

  • 01 A wider Market

    What more could a regular farmer desire than the ability to broaden there market, Adwar Agrolink is providing the perfect platform to connect the farmer to the market.

  • 02 Fellowship community

    This platform also provides a brotherhood community for our users in form of blogs as well as a chat room to connect our various users.

  • 03 Training

    We also connect our people to free online courses in the crop farming sector to enable then gain further knowledge in the sector

  • 04 Inhouse services

    We also connect our clients to inhouse services for example counselling, whether legal, or otherwise we have got ypu covered.
